# Energy Transition 2025: Renewable Energy Adoption Trends

## Executive Summary

The global energy transition is accelerating, with renewable energy sources gaining significant market share. This brief analyzes adoption trends, policy frameworks, and strategic implications for the energy sector.

## Global Renewable Energy Landscape

Renewable energy now accounts for over 30% of global electricity generation, up from 20% just five years ago. Solar and wind power are leading the transition, with costs falling dramatically.

### Key Statistics

- **Solar Capacity**: 1.2 TW globally, growing at 25% annually
- **Wind Capacity**: 900 GW globally, with offshore wind expanding rapidly
- **Energy Storage**: 200 GW of battery storage capacity, critical for grid stability
- **Investment**: $500B+ annual investment in renewable energy infrastructure
